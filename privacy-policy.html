数据收集 · Data Collection

本 App 不收集任何个人信息，不连接服务器，不追踪用户行为。

This app does not collect any personal information. It does not connect to any server or track user behavior.

本地存储 · Local Storage

练习记录（正确率、用时）仅保存在您的设备本地（UserDefaults），不上传至任何服务器。

Practice records (accuracy, time) are saved locally on your device only (UserDefaults) and are never uploaded to any server.

付费购买 · In-App Purchase

App 内提供"一次性买断"和"自动续期年度订阅"两种解锁方式，购买均通过 Apple App Store 完成，我们不接触或存储任何支付信息。

This app offers a one-time unlock purchase and an auto-renewable annual subscription. Both are processed entirely by Apple's App Store; we do not access or store any payment information.

自动续期订阅说明 · Auto-Renewable Subscription Terms

· 订阅费用将在确认购买时从您的 Apple ID 账户中扣款；
· 订阅会自动续期，除非在当前订阅周期结束前至少 24 小时关闭自动续期；
· 续期费用将在当前周期结束前 24 小时内扣款，续期价格与当前订阅价格一致；
· 您可以在购买后随时前往「设置」→ 您的 Apple ID → 「订阅」管理或取消订阅，取消不会影响当前订阅周期内已付费的使用权限；
· 如订阅包含免费试用，购买同一订阅将作废任何剩余的免费试用期（如适用）。

· Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the confirmation of purchase;
· Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period;
· Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period, at the same price as the current subscription;
· You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions; cancellation will not affect your access for the remainder of the period you already paid for;
· Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when you purchase a subscription, where applicable.
