TrueBearing Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-31

Summary

TrueBearing collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and contains no analytics or advertising.

Data Collection and Storage

The app requests three permissions, all retained locally:

  • Location (GPS): Used for speed, coordinates, altitude, and odometer
  • Motion & Fitness: Enables barometric altitude via Core Motion
  • Camera: Powers torch functionality (flashlight, strobe, SOS modes)

All data stays on your device with no uploading or third-party sharing.

No Tracking

TrueBearing contains no analytics SDK, no advertising framework, and no crash-reporting service that phones home.

In-App Purchases

TrueBearing Pro is available as an auto-renewable yearly subscription (with a 7-day free trial) or a one-time lifetime purchase. Apple StoreKit handles all transactions exclusively — the developer receives no payment details, email, or personal information. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and can be managed or cancelled anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Apple's policies apply.

Data Removal

Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data automatically.

Children

The app does not intentionally collect information from minors or any users.

Policy Updates

Changes appear at this URL with an updated effective date.

Support

For questions or concerns, visit: https://github.com/SolrFire/4Apple

TrueBearing 隐私政策

生效日期：2026-05-31

概述

TrueBearing 不收集任何个人数据，不向任何服务器发送数据，也不含任何分析或广告。

数据收集与存储

本应用请求三项权限，全部仅保存在本地：

  • 位置（GPS）：用于速度、坐标、海拔与里程
  • 运动与健身：通过 Core Motion 提供气压海拔
  • 相机：提供手电筒功能（手电、频闪、SOS 模式）

所有数据均保留在你的设备上，不会上传，也不会与第三方共享。

无追踪

TrueBearing 不含任何分析 SDK、广告框架，也没有任何会回传数据的崩溃报告服务。

内购

TrueBearing Pro 提供按年自动续订订阅（含 7 天免费试用）或一次性永久购买。所有交易完全由 Apple StoreKit 处理——开发者不会收到任何付款信息、邮箱或个人信息。订阅会自动续订，除非在当前订阅期结束前至少 24 小时取消；可随时在「设置 > Apple ID > 订阅」中管理或取消。适用 Apple 的相关政策。

数据删除

卸载应用会自动删除所有本地存储的数据。

儿童

本应用不会有意收集未成年人或任何用户的信息。

政策更新

任何变更都会发布在此网址，并更新生效日期。

支持

如有疑问或顾虑，请访问：https://github.com/SolrFire/4Apple
