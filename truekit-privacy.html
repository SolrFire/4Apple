TrueKit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TrueKit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use TrueKit.

Data We Do NOT Collect

TrueKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No data sent to our servers

Device Permissions

Some tools require device permissions, used only locally on your device:

  • Camera — Used by Magnifier, AR Ruler, and Protractor camera mode. Images are never stored or transmitted.
  • Microphone — Used by Sound Meter to measure ambient noise levels in real time. Audio is never recorded or stored.
  • Motion & Orientation — Used by Bubble Level to detect device tilt. Data stays on device.

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

Currency Exchange Rates

The Currency tool fetches exchange rates from a public API (frankfurter.app). No personal data is sent with this request.

Purchase Data

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

TrueKit does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
